UKCI Conference 2024
4th - 6th September
Ulster University, Belfast, Northern Ireland

Fees


Early Bird Registration Open Until: 22nd July 2024
Early Bird Registration: £325
Regular Registration: £375
Early Bird Student Registration: £225
Student Registration: £275


Registration


You can register for UKCI 2024 through Ulster University's Online Store when registration opens (opening soon).

We are planning for an in-person conference. However, we may be able to accommodate online participation under exceptional circumstances for those who are not able to travel (in particular from overseas). We will publish relevant guidance on the website in due course. Should you have any concerns or reservations about travelling but still wish to participate, please email the conference organisers.

All accepted papers must be registered with either a regular or student registration prior to the submission of the camera-ready version. The registration includes attendance at all technical sessions, and access to proceedings.
Please note:

  • Student Registration - covers only one paper.
  • Regular Registration - covers a maximum of two papers with a common co-author.

    • The deadline for an early bird registration is: 22nd July 2024
